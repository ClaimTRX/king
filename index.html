The Weekly KING Voting System

Every week, holders can compete for the title of "King" by paying a small fee in $KING tokens. Voters choose their preferred candidate, and the winner earns 100% of the 5% tax from $KING transactions during their reign. The more $KING is traded, the greater the rewards for the King.
