About the King Token

The King Token is your gateway to the kingdom. Each month, holders of King Token have the power to vote for their favorite candidate to be crowned "King of the Week." The crowned King will receive the entire 5% buy tax collected from all transactions during the week, making it a highly sought-after title!

Register on our website with your name and wallet address to participate in the voting and stand a chance to become the King of the Month. Each vote costs a certain amount of King Tokens, so use your tokens wisely and make your voice heard in the kingdom!
